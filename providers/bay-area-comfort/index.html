About Bay Area Comfort HVAC

Bay Area Comfort HVAC is a full-service heating and air conditioning contractor based in Alameda, CA. With over 12 years of experience serving East Bay homeowners, they specialize in heat pump installations, furnace replacements, and energy-efficient upgrades for older homes.

Owner-operated and locally trusted, Bay Area Comfort helps homeowners navigate rebates and incentives for energy-efficient upgrades. They're known for clean installations, transparent pricing, and expertise with the unique challenges of older East Bay homes.
