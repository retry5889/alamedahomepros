About Bay Plumbing Co.

Bay Plumbing Co. is a full-service plumbing contractor based in Alameda, CA. With over 15 years of experience serving East Bay homeowners, they specialize in the unique challenges of older homes — from repiping corroded galvanized steel to replacing cast iron sewer lines.

Owner-operated and locally trusted, Bay Plumbing handles everything from emergency repairs to planned renovations. They're known for honest diagnoses, clean work, and showing up when they say they will.

Services Offered

  • Emergency plumbing repair (burst pipes, major leaks)
  • Drain cleaning (kitchen, bathroom, main line)
  • Water heater replacement (tank and tankless)
  • Sewer line inspection, repair, and replacement
  • Whole-house repiping (galvanized to copper or PEX)
  • Fixture installation (faucets, toilets, garbage disposals)
  • Gas line detection and repair

Service Area

Alameda, Oakland, and surrounding East Bay cities. Primary focus on Alameda's residential neighborhoods.
