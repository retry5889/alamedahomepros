About Alameda Electric Co.

Alameda Electric Co. is a full-service electrical contractor based in Alameda, CA. With over 20 years of experience serving East Bay homeowners, they specialize in panel upgrades, EV charger installation, and whole-house rewiring for pre-1980 homes.

Owner-operated and locally trusted, Alameda Electric handles everything from emergency repairs to planned electrical upgrades. They're known for clean installations, thorough work, and helping homeowners navigate the electrical needs of older Bay Area homes.

Services Offered

  • Electrical panel upgrades (100A to 200A+)
  • EV charger installation (Tesla, ChargePoint, all brands)
  • Whole-house rewiring for pre-1980 homes
  • Outlet and switch installation
  • Ceiling fan and light fixture installation
  • Emergency electrical repair
  • Electrical safety inspections
  • Code compliance upgrades

Service Area

Alameda, Oakland, Berkeley, and surrounding East Bay cities. Primary focus on Alameda's residential neighborhoods.
